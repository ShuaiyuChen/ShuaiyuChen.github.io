Welcome! My name is Shuaiyu Chen.

I’m an Assistant Professor of Finance at Mitchell E. Daniels, Jr. School of Business, Purdue University.

My research interests include empirical asset pricing, asset management, short selling and securities lending, financial news, and return predictability.

Shuaiyu Chen

Assistant Professor of Finance,

Purdue University

I do theoretically-motivated empirical research on the following topics:

  1. The informational efficiency of security prices and return predictability
  2. The performance of fund managers and the behavior of fund flows
  3. Short selling and securities lending
  4. Financial news and social media in financial markets
